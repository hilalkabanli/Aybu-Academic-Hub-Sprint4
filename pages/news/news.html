Equine Therapy for the Elderly at AYBU

Date: March 23, 2024

AYBU Hippotherapy Application and Research Center organized a special event for the elderly residents coming from Abidin Yılmaz Nursing Home and Rehabilitation Center in Çubuk district as part of the annual Elderly Week celebrations. The event was aimed at supporting the physical and mental health of the elderly through activities focusing on equine therapy. Equine therapy is an effective treatment method that can provide many benefits for elderly individuals, such as reducing stress, increasing muscle strength, and improving balance. During this event, elderly participants interacted with horses, learned about horses, and enjoyed their time. Under the guidance of Hippotherapy Center Director Lecturer Ali Ekber ÜN and Instructor Burcu ÇALIK BAĞRIYANIK, students provided information to the elderly about horse living areas and demonstrated how to care for horses. Additionally, grooming materials for horses were introduced, and practical information about horse care was shared. Elderly participants took guided tours with students and experienced horseback riding. Through these activities, they both remained physically active and mentally relaxed through their interaction with the horses.

Disaster Awareness Training Conducted by Civil Defense and Firefighting Program Students

Date: March 20, 2024

Our University's School of Social Sciences Vocational School students from the Civil Defense and Firefighting program conducted informative training on earthquakes, floods, and fire disasters at the Esenboğa campus. Within the scope of the program, detailed presentations were made on measures to be taken against disasters and what to do before, during, and after disasters. Practical information was provided to students on how to act in disaster situations and how to ensure safety.
